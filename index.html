FEATURES

Why Choose Us

Experienced PvP Players

Empire has its fair share of experienced PvP players. We pride ourselves on being a PvP guild, so it is a requirement that you must have at least 50 rating to join. You must also maintain that rating throughout the month. If you are on hiatus for more than 1 month, you will be kicked. You may still have access to the Empire Discord, however. Many members can attest that they have improved massively since joining Empire, and if our player base continues to grow, we could easily get access to Guild Island, an exclusive island specifically for the Top 3 guilds..

We help new players familiarize themselves with both PvE and PvP

If you aren't too crazy about PvP and enjoy the other aspects of the game like Bosses, Quests, Hunting, et cetera, we have a ton of helpful resources and events, which can help make all of these tasks easier.

We are a strong, loyal, and trustworthy community

Our community is something we've been developing more and more as time goes on. I'd say the community is one of our strong points. Many of Empire's members have remained since the beginning of its creation. It goes without saying that many people love it here, mainly for the social aspect. There are regular voice calls, and daily interaction with guild members is common. We've all become bonded over time and our friendships will only continue to grow with the addition of new members to our community.

The Empire

Empire, despite being a relatively new guild, has managed to reach the Top 10 on the Guild Ladder countless times, solidifying our position as one of the top guilds. Many of our core members are dedicated PvP players who have years of experience and actively work their hardest to help newer players get a better understanding of the game. There are many designated roles for each person. If you show that, over time, you are making significant contributions to the guild, you will be rewarded with roles that represent your contributions. We encourage debate and discussion about pressing metagame topics, and no opinions (no matter how trivial) will go ignored. Overall, we strive for a healthy balance of fun and competitiveness and you will undoubtedly learn new things upon joining our guild.
